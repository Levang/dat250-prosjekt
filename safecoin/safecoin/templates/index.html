About SafeCoin

Safecoin is a bank where you can safely store and transfer your money. Not much else really...

Google Authenticator

Safecoin requires the use of Google Authenticator for two-factor authentication. You will use this like you would use a code-generator from another bank. The app can be downloaded in the Google Play Store and in the Apple App Store.

Password

In this bank you are required to use a password that is at least 12 characters long. The password should be unique and not contain phrases of information that is easily obtainable, e.g. - names, common words, dates etc... To comply with this, it is recommended that you use a password-manager such as Bitwarden, Dashlane or Lastpass. There you can create long and difficult to guess password for each of your online account, while only having to remember one password.
